
# 🏥 AI Medical Assistant

> An intelligent AI-powered medical companion built with **FastAPI**, **Streamlit**, and **Google Gemini** to provide instant health insights and guidance.

---

## ✨ Overview

Get instant AI-generated medical insights in seconds. Simply describe your symptoms and receive:

- 🔍 **Possible Causes** – AI-identified potential conditions
- 💊 **Home Remedies** – Natural and practical remedies
- 👨‍⚕️ **Doctor Consultation Advice** – When and how to seek professional help
- ⚠️ **Emergency Warning Signs** – Critical symptoms requiring immediate care

**⚕️ Medical Disclaimer:** *This tool provides educational information only and is not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or treatment.*
